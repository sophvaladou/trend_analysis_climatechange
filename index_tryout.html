Uncovering Twitter trends with BERTopic:
a case study on climate change conversations

This website is used for the presentation of the plots related to the master's thesis of Sophia Valadou. This research encompasses a trend analysis on Twitter using the BERTopic model on climate change-related tweets. Since the plots are often too crowded for visualization, we opted for interactive plots instead. By clicking the legend on each plot three times, all trend lines disappear. Then, the trend lines that one wishes to display can be selected by clicking them once. This way, the plots are not too crowded and only the desired trend lines are displayed.
